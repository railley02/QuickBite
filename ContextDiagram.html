Key System Features Illustrated:

  • Real-time Inventory Management: Atomic updates prevent overselling
  • Time Slot Management: Fair distribution of pickup times within capacity limits
  • Digital Queue System: Eliminates physical waiting and ensures order fairness
  • Payment Integration: Secure payment processing with multiple options
  • Push Notifications: Real-time updates on order status
  • Staff Dashboard: Efficient order management for cafeteria staff
  • Analytics & Reporting: Insights for better cafeteria operations
